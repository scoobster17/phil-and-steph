Wedding Day Details

The big event upcoming in July 2017 is set. Oh no, you've got to start worrying about what to wear. What everyone else will be wearing. What they serve at the bar. Oh dear. Here you can find out all you need to know for the wedding day...

Who's Invited

Invitations are yet to be sent out. Only guests who receive an invitation are permitted to attend the wedding ceremony and/or reception.

Please note that we would like a nice, much smaller, more intimate gathering for our wedding, and to this end we are not inviting everyone that was invited to the engagement party.

The idea with the engagement party was to have an event where we could celebrate with everyone our proposed marriage.

Where to go

Three Rivers Golf and Country Club; a beautiful and glorious green blanket of grass lays background to a fairly modern country club building.

There are rooms at the venue, however these have been pre-booked by the bride and groom and pre-allocated to immediate family and the bridal party etc. Therefore it is advised that if you wish to stay over near the venue you should book a room nearby.
